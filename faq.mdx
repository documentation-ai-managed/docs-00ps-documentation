## Account and Login Problems

You encounter login issues on Baidu's platform from time to time. Common causes include forgotten passwords, browser cache problems, or network restrictions. Use the steps below to resolve most account-related problems.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Reset Password" icon="key" href="#reset-password">
    Recover access quickly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Enable 2FA" icon="shield" href="#enable-2fa">
    Add extra security.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Contact Support" icon="message-circle" href="https://support.baidu.com" target="_blank">
    Get personalized help.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="How do I reset my forgotten password?" default-open="true">
    Navigate to the login page and click "Forgot Password?". Enter your registered email address. Baidu sends a reset link valid for 24 hours. Follow the link to set a new password meeting these requirements: at least 8 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, and a number.

    <Callout kind="tip">
      Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) after resetting to prevent future issues.
    </Callout>
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What if I cannot log in after reset?">
    Clear your browser cache and cookies. Switch to an incognito window or another browser like Chrome or Firefox.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Clear Cache" icon="trash-2">
        Press <kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>Shift</kbd>+<kbd>Delete</kbd> (Windows) or <kbd>Cmd</kbd>+<kbd>Shift</kbd>+<kbd>Delete</kbd> (macOS). Select "Cookies and cache" and clear for the past hour.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Try Incognito" icon="incognito">
        Open a new incognito tab and attempt login again.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check Network" icon="globe">
        Disable VPN or proxy if active, then retry.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

## Search Optimization Tips

Improve your search results on Baidu by using advanced operators and structured data. These techniques help you retrieve precise information faster.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Search" icon="globe">
    Use operators like quotes for exact matches: `"machine learning algorithms"`.

    <CodeGroup tabs="Operators,Advanced">
      ```text
      site:baidu.com AI tools
      filetype:pdf research paper
      inurl:docs tutorial
      ```
      ```text
      "exact phrase" AND (AI OR artificial intelligence)
      date:>2024-01-01 recent updates
      related:baidu.com similar sites
      ```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="API Integration" icon="code">
    For developers, optimize queries via Baidu's Search API.

    ```javascript
    const query = 'AI optimization tips';
    const url = `https://api.baidu.com/search?q=${encodeURIComponent(query)}&count=10`;
    const response = await fetch(url, {
      headers: { 'Authorization': `Bearer YOUR_API_TOKEN` }
    });
    const results = await response.json();
    console.log(results);
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="success">
  Regularly update your search preferences in account settings for personalized results.
</Callout>

## Privacy and Data Usage

Baidu prioritizes your privacy. You control what data we collect and how it is used.

| Topic | Details | Your Control |
|-------|---------|--------------|
| Search History | Stored to improve recommendations | Delete via settings or incognito mode |
| Location Data | Used for local results | Disable in profile > Privacy |
| Personalized Ads | Based on search patterns | Opt out in Advertising preferences |

<Expandable title="How does Baidu handle my data?">
  We anonymize search queries and retain logs for 30 days maximum. No personal data is sold to third parties. Review our full policy at baidu.com/privacy.

  ```mermaid
  graph TD
      A[Your Search] --> B[Anonymized Query]
      B --> C[Process Results]
      C --> D[Return to You]
      E[Logs] --> F[Delete after 30 days]
  ```
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Can I delete my data?">
  Yes. Go to account settings > Data Management > Delete History. For full account deletion, contact support with verification.
</Expandable>
