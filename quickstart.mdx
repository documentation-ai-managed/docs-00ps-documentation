## Create Your Baidu Account

Follow these steps to set up your free Baidu account. You gain access to personalized searches, saved results, and premium services.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Baidu" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and go to [www.baidu.com](https://www.baidu.com).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Locate the "Sign Up" button in the top-right corner and click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="edit">
    Provide your email or phone number, create a strong password, and complete verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Account" icon="check-circle">
    Check your email or SMS for the verification code and enter it to activate.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Use a strong password combining letters, numbers, and symbols for better security.
</Callout>

## Perform Your First Search

Query Baidu's vast index of web content. Choose your preferred method below.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Browser" icon="globe">
    Enter your search term like "Beijing weather" in the search box on [www.baidu.com](https://www.baidu.com). Press Enter to see instant results with snippets, images, and links.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile App" icon="smartphone">
    Download the Baidu App from App Store or Google Play. Log in with your account and tap the search icon to query on the go.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="API" icon="code">
    Integrate searches programmatically. First, generate an API key from your Baidu account dashboard.

    <CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,Python,cURL">
      ```javascript
      const API_KEY = 'your-baidu-api-key';
      const query = 'Beijing weather';
      
      fetch(`https://api.baidu.com/search?q=${encodeURIComponent(query)}&key=${API_KEY}`)
        .then(response => response.json())
        .then(data => console.log(data.results));
      ```

      ```python
      import requests
      
      API_KEY = 'your-baidu-api-key'
      query = 'Beijing weather'
      
      response = requests.get(
          'https://api.baidu.com/search',
          params={'q': query, 'key': API_KEY}
      )
      print(response.json()['results'])
      ```

      ```bash
      curl "https://api.baidu.com/search?q=Beijing%20weather&key=your-baidu-api-key"
      ```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
